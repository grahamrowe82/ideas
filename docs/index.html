Along the arc

The publication is named for a change of state, and it has been through a few.

  1. 2025

    Philosophical, cognitive

    Short pieces about working with your own mind and with AI.

  2. late 2025

    Bridging, factory thinking

    The frame shifts from inner cognition to operational systems. AI as a factory, not a brain.

  3. 2026

    Technical, practical

    The output explodes. Deep agent and MCP work, grounded in real data.

Beyond the writing

Beyond the writing, I work with teams to connect AI to the data and systems their business already runs on. Retainers, fixed-scope projects, and partnerships that turn "we should try AI" into systems that do real work.
